Cosmetics Store

In this game you are working in the store and your goal is to make as much profit as you can.

When customer calls you, run to her and press SPACE to serve her. After a little chat, costumer will buy product and give you money or just walk away.

If she gives you money, you need to go to the register and put money in it by pressing SPACE. Then, you can serve next costumer.

Some costumers are more likely to buy something, some spend more money etc.

You have 3 minutes. Enter your name to begin and good luck!
